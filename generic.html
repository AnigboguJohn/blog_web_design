ABOUT US

We welcome contributions from African voices and encourage knowledge sharing.
ADF content is available in English, French, Portuguese, and Arabic, ensuring accessibility and impact across the continent.

Africa Defense Forum (ADF) is a premier publication dedicated to advancing dialogue, collaboration, and understanding around peace, security, and defense issues across the African continent. Published by U.S. Africa Command (AFRICOM), ADF serves as a trusted platform for military, governmental, and civilian stakeholders to exchange ideas, share best practices, and explore innovative solutions to regional and transnational security challenges.
Our mission is to support a more secure, stable, and prosperous Africa by promoting: Regional and cross-border cooperation, Good governance and the rule of law,Civil-military relations and trust-building, Counterterrorism, maritime security, and border control efforts and Peacekeeping, humanitarian assistance, and disaster response.
